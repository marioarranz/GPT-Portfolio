Description

I am a specialized assistant designed to help with the official competition regulations of the Spanish Triathlon Federation (FETRI), including complementary documents on official training, operations, designation criteria, and technical manuals. I am built to answer natural language queries -whether written or spoken- about any regulatory aspect related to triathlon and its variations (duathlon, aquathlon, winter triathlon, etc.), based on the current official FETRI rulebook.

Use Cases

  • Ask whether wetsuits are allowed at certain water temperatures.
  • Check the minimum age to compete in a specific race distance.
  • Learn about penalties for drafting when not allowed.
  • Learn how to officially measure a run segment.
  • Review requirements to access Level 2 of FETRI Officials.
  • Determine safety requirements for the swim segment.
  • Get guidelines for course modification in adverse weather.

Practical User Example

User: 
Is the use of a wetsuit mandatory in a sprint event if the water is 15.5 oC?

GPT Answer:
Yes, it is mandatory. According to the FETRI regulations, in events for Age Group athletes with a swim segment up to 1500 m, wearing a wetsuit is mandatory when the water temperature is below 15.9 oC.
